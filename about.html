Our Story

Built on a Foundation
of Trust & Dedication

Uneek Property Management & Real Estate was founded with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap between property owners seeking reliable management and tenants looking for quality homes in Ghana. What began as a small agency has grown into a comprehensive property services firm trusted by hundreds of clients across Accra and beyond.

Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include building & construction, professional cleaning services, and investment advisory — becoming a true one-stop shop for all things property. Our growth is a reflection of the trust our clients place in us and the unwavering commitment of our team.

Mission, Vision & Values

The guiding principles that define who we are and how we serve every client, every day.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional, transparent, and reliable property management and real estate services that protect our clients' investments, create great tenant experiences, and contribute to the development of quality living and working spaces across Ghana.

Our Vision

To be Ghana's most trusted and respected property management and real estate company — recognised for our integrity, innovation, and commitment to excellence in every property we touch and every client we serve.

Core Values

  • Integrity — Honest, transparent dealings in everything we do
  • Excellence — Delivering quality above and beyond expectations
  • Accountability — Owning our results and our commitments
  • Innovation — Embracing better ways to serve our clients
  • Client Focus — Putting our clients' needs at the centre
